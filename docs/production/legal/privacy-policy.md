# Opwan API Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Opwan API ("Opwan," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data when you use our website, dashboard, APIs, prepaid credits, fixed-term service plans, support channels, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Data we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:

- **Account data:** username, email address, authentication information, account role, settings, and verification status.
- **Payment and transaction data:** products or credits purchased, amount, currency, order identifiers, payment status, billing country, tax information, and limited payment-method details supplied by the payment provider. We do not receive or store complete payment-card numbers.
- **API and usage data:** API key or token identifiers, selected model, timestamps, request status, token or media usage, quota consumed, latency, channel and routing metadata, and error information.
- **Submitted content:** prompts, files, images, audio, video, tool inputs, and other content that you ask us to transmit or process. Content is processed as needed to route requests, provide outputs, prevent abuse, troubleshoot failures, and comply with law. Retention may depend on the feature, logging configuration, support request, and selected provider.
- **Device and network data:** IP address, browser and device type, operating system, referring URL, session information, security events, and cookie or similar identifiers.
- **Support and communications data:** messages, attachments, feedback, and other information you provide when contacting us.

## 2. How we use data

We use personal data to:

- create and secure accounts, authenticate users, and prevent unauthorized access;
- provide, route, meter, and improve API and dashboard functionality;
- calculate usage, process orders, manage credits and service plans, issue receipts, and support refunds;
- monitor reliability, debug errors, prevent fraud and abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service;
- respond to support requests and send transactional or service-related communications;
- comply with legal obligations and valid requests from authorities; and
- establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we process data to perform our contract with you, pursue legitimate interests such as security and service improvement, comply with law, and, where required, based on consent.

## 3. How we share data

We may disclose data to the following recipients only as reasonably necessary:

- **AI model and infrastructure providers:** submitted content and request metadata are transmitted to the provider or route selected for your request. Provider terms and privacy practices may apply.
- **Payment providers:** Waffo Pancake or another provider identified at checkout processes payments, fraud checks, tax, receipts, plan purchases, and refunds. Waffo's privacy policy is available at [waffo.com/privacy-policy](https://waffo.com/privacy-policy).
- **Hosting, security, monitoring, email, and support vendors:** these vendors process data on our behalf to operate the Service.
- **Professional advisers and authorities:** where reasonably necessary for legal, accounting, compliance, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.
- **Business transfers:** in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality and notice requirements.

We do not sell personal data for money. We do not share personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## 4. International processing

The Service and its providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we use contractual or other lawful safeguards for international transfers. By selecting a third-party model provider, you understand that submitted content may be processed where that provider operates.

## 5. Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, including account operation, billing, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

Retention periods vary by data type. Transaction and accounting records may be retained for the period required by tax and financial law. Security and usage logs are retained according to operational need and configured retention settings. Submitted content is retained only when required by the selected feature, provider, troubleshooting process, abuse investigation, or law. Deleting your account does not require us to delete records that must be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

## 6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect data, including access controls, encrypted transport, credential protections, logging, and service monitoring. No system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Keep your account credentials and API keys confidential and contact us promptly if you suspect compromise.

## 7. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of personal data, obtain a portable copy, or withdraw consent. You may update certain account information in the dashboard and may submit a request to [support@opwan.ai](mailto:support@opwan.ai).

We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. Rights may be limited where an exception applies, including security, fraud prevention, legal claims, or mandatory record retention. You may also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

## 8. Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and browser storage that are necessary for authentication, security, language and theme preferences, session continuity, and core Service functionality. Where optional analytics or similar technologies require consent, we will request it before use.

## 9. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children who cannot legally consent to data processing in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from such children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal data without appropriate authorization.

## 10. Third-party links and services

The Service may link to or interoperate with third-party websites, model providers, and developer tools. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy. Review those policies before sending sensitive or regulated information.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, the Service, or applicable law. The updated version will show a new effective date. We will provide additional notice for material changes where required.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions or rights requests, email [support@opwan.ai](mailto:support@opwan.ai). Please do not include API keys, passwords, full payment-card details, or unnecessary sensitive content in your message.
