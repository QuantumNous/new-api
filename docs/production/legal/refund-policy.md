# Opwan API Refund Policy

**Effective date: July 18, 2026**

This Refund Policy applies to prepaid credits, one-time purchases, and fixed-term service plans purchased from Opwan API. It forms part of the [Opwan API Terms of Service](/user-agreement).

## 1. Fourteen-day request window

You may request a refund within **14 calendar days** after the applicable payment. A request made after that period is normally not eligible unless required by law or approved because of a duplicate charge, unauthorized payment, or confirmed billing or service-delivery error.

## 2. Prepaid credits and one-time purchases

- Unused paid credits are eligible for a refund when requested within the 14-day window.
- If part of the purchased credits has been consumed, we may issue a partial refund for the unused paid portion. The refundable amount is calculated from the actual price paid after discounts.
- Promotional, bonus, referral, trial, or otherwise free credits have no cash value and are not refundable.
- Usage already delivered, including completed model inference, generated media, or other metered service, is not refundable unless the charge resulted from a confirmed system billing error.

## 3. Fixed-term service plans

Plans purchased through Waffo Pancake are one-time purchases for the duration and quota shown before payment. They do not automatically renew, and buying another term requires a new checkout.

A plan purchase may be refunded within 14 days if no material plan usage occurred after the charge. If material usage occurred, the charge is normally non-refundable except where required by law or where we confirm a duplicate charge, unauthorized payment, or service-delivery failure. We do not provide prorated refunds for unused time in an activated plan unless required by law.

## 4. Duplicate, unauthorized, or incorrect charges

Contact us promptly if you believe a payment was duplicated, unauthorized, incorrectly calculated, or completed without the purchased credits or plan being delivered. We will investigate using account, order, usage, and payment-provider records. Nothing in this policy limits rights provided by applicable consumer law.

## 5. How to request a refund

Email [support@opwan.ai](mailto:support@opwan.ai) from the address associated with your account and include:

- your account email;
- the order or transaction identifier shown on your receipt;
- the payment date and amount;
- whether you request a full or partial refund; and
- a brief reason for the request.

Do not send passwords, API keys, or full payment-card details. We may ask for additional information reasonably necessary to verify account ownership or investigate the payment.

## 6. Review and processing

We aim to acknowledge refund requests within **2 business days**. Approved refunds are submitted to the original payment method through Waffo Pancake or the payment provider shown on your receipt.

After approval, refunds usually appear within **5–10 business days**, but timing depends on the payment provider, card network, bank, currency, and country. We cannot send a card payment refund to a different card, bank account, or person. Applicable taxes are adjusted by the payment provider where required.

## 7. Abuse and account restrictions

We may deny a refund request that is fraudulent, abusive, materially misrepresents usage, or attempts to retain the benefit of consumed service. A policy violation does not remove any non-waivable consumer rights. We may suspend access while investigating fraud, chargebacks, or account compromise.

Before starting a chargeback, please contact us so we can investigate and, where appropriate, issue a refund through the original payment channel. This does not limit any right you have to dispute a payment with your provider.

## 8. Contact

Refund questions and requests should be sent to [support@opwan.ai](mailto:support@opwan.ai).
